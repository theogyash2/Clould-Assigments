1. Sign in to AWS Console:
2. Navigate to DynamoDB Dashboard:
3. In the DynamoDB Dashboard, click on the "Create table" button.
Enter "BookDB" as the Table name.
Specify "bookid" as the Partition key and "bookname" as the Sort key (since bookid is unique and bookname can have duplicates).
Click on the "Create" button to create your table.
4. Add Items to the Table:
Once your "BookDB" table is created, you can add items to it.
Click on the "Items" tab in your table's details page and then click on the "Create item" button.
Enter the attribute values for each item, including "bookid," "bookname," and "author," and click on the "Save" button.
5. You will be able to see the DB from the Dynamo DB dashboard.
